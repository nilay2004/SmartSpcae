Common Questions

FAQ

Can't find your answer? Reach us at smartspace@gmail.com or +91 98690 35747.

How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by scope. A single-room interior design takes 4–6 weeks. A full-home renovation typically runs 4–8 months.
Do you work outside Mumbai?
Yes. We regularly take on projects across Maharashtra — Pune, Nashik, Alibaug, and Goa. Travel costs beyond 60km are billed separately.
Can I see physical material samples?
Absolutely. We arrange physical sample boards for all key materials — tiles, stone, timber, fabrics — before any purchase order is placed.
Is there a warranty on the construction?
Yes. All structural and fit-out work carries a 1-year defect liability period. Signature clients receive 1-year post-handover support.
